KPSS Soru Deneme 2026 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ronai Labs handles information when users install or use KPSS Soru Deneme 2026, a mobile education and exam-preparation app for KPSS candidates.

The app is designed for study, practice, and progress tracking. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to OSYM.

1. Overview

KPSS Soru Deneme 2026 provides original KPSS-style practice questions, mock exams, concept cards, word-hunt games, and local progress tracking. The app may show ads through Google AdMob and may use platform services needed for app operation, diagnostics, and advertising compliance.

2. Information we may handle

2.1 Information stored on the device

  • Selected avatar and display name, if the user customizes the profile.
  • Practice progress, completed topics, score history, answer state, hint usage, and similar learning progress data.
  • App preferences such as selected section, sound state, and interface-related choices.

This learning progress is primarily stored locally on the user's device. If the app is deleted, locally stored progress may also be deleted unless the operating system restores app data from a backup.

2.2 Information collected automatically by third-party services

  • Device and app information such as device type, operating system, app version, language, and approximate region.
  • Advertising identifiers or similar identifiers where allowed by the platform and user settings.
  • Ad interaction and delivery data used to show, measure, limit, and improve advertising.
  • Diagnostics, crash, or performance information if provided by platform or service providers.

3. Advertising and AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads, including interstitial and rewarded ads. AdMob and related Google services may collect and process data as described in Google's own policies, including advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, ad interactions, and diagnostics.

Users can control certain ad personalization settings through their device settings and Google account settings where available.

4. Local progress and gameplay state

The app may save progress so users can continue from where they left off. This may include solved questions, selected answers, scores, completed topics, word-hunt completion state, and similar usage information. This information helps the app show progress bars, completion status, and score screens.

5. Educational content and OSYM notice

KPSS Soru Deneme 2026 is an independent educational app. The app is not an official OSYM product and has no official partnership with OSYM. Content is intended for KPSS preparation and is prepared as original or KPSS-style educational material.

Users should treat app content as study support, not as an official exam source or guarantee of exam performance.

6. How we use information

  • To provide study features, quizzes, games, score screens, and progress tracking.
  • To remember app preferences and improve user experience.
  • To display ads and support rewarded hint flows.
  • To monitor app reliability, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • To comply with app-store, advertising, legal, and safety obligations.

7. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate the app, including advertising, analytics, diagnostics, hosting, or app-store platform services. These providers process information under their own policies and applicable contractual terms.

8. Children and age suitability

The app is intended for KPSS candidates and general education use. It is not directed to young children. If applicable law requires parental consent for a user's age group, the user should use the app only with appropriate authorization.

9. User choices and deletion

  • Users can delete app-local progress by removing the app from the device, subject to device backup behavior.
  • Users can manage ad personalization through device and Google settings where available.
  • Users may contact us for privacy questions or deletion requests related to support communications.

10. Security and retention

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for the type of information handled. Local progress remains on the device until deleted by the user, reset by app controls where available, or removed by uninstalling the app. Information processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers' policies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice through the app, this website, or app-store metadata where appropriate.
