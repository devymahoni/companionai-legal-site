SeriesGuru Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ronai Labs handles information in SeriesGuru, a mobile quiz app for TV series fans.

Contents

  1. Overview
  2. Information we handle
  3. How we use information
  4. Third-party services
  5. Retention and deletion
  6. Children's privacy
  7. Contact

1. Overview

SeriesGuru lets users discover quiz packs, answer multiple-choice questions, earn points, and appear on leaderboards. The app is an independent fan quiz app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any TV network, streaming platform, production company, or rights holder.

2. Information we handle

  • Account information, such as display name, email address, user identifier, and profile image when users sign in with Google or Apple.
  • Gameplay information, such as score, completed quizzes, unlocked progress, and leaderboard entries.
  • Technical information needed to run the app, diagnose issues, protect the service, and improve reliability.
  • Support messages and privacy requests users send to Ronai Labs.

3. How we use information

  • To provide sign-in, quiz progress, scoring, and leaderboard features.
  • To keep user progress available across supported devices where account-based features are used.
  • To respond to support, privacy, account, or safety requests.
  • To maintain app security, prevent misuse, debug issues, and improve app quality.

4. Third-party services

SeriesGuru may use third-party services that support app operation, including authentication, database hosting, app distribution, crash diagnostics, analytics, and platform services. These providers process data according to their own policies and applicable agreements.

SeriesGuru may also display publicly available TV metadata or imagery from third-party data sources where applicable. This content is used to support discovery and quiz context.

5. Retention and deletion

Account and leaderboard data is retained while an account is active or as needed to provide app features. Users may request account or data deletion by contacting Ronai Labs. Some information may be retained for a limited period when required for security, fraud prevention, billing, legal compliance, or backup processing.

Account deletion instructions are available on the Account Deletion page.

6. Children's privacy

SeriesGuru is intended for a general audience unless otherwise specified in the app store listing. Ronai Labs does not knowingly collect personal information from children below the age required by applicable law without appropriate authorization.
